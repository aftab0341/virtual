PAKISTAN AIR FORCE MUSEUMLocated adjacent to the PAF Base Faisal on the main Shahrah-e-Faisal, PAF Museum was inaugurated on 14th August, 1997 by the then Chief of the Air Staff Air Chief Marshal Abbas Khattak; and subsequently opened to the general public in October, 1997.

Pakistan Air Force has a proud history of sacrifice valour and professionalism. Our brave sons of the soil have always responded to the call of the nation with unparalleled courage for a cause greater than life defence of Motherland. PAF holds its Shuhada and war veterans in highest esteem as they sacrificed their today for our tomorrow. This monument symbolizes patriotism and pay rich tributes to our Shuhada and it will serve as lasting reminder of their dauntless courage and matchless heroism. Each name engraved on this monument is a complete chapter in itself of PAF’s illustrious history in all its grandeur as this monument is not merely a structure of bricks rather it will help instil the heroic spirit of our Shuhada in our future generations by fuelling their passion for sacrifice their nationhood and professionalism.
